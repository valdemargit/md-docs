### interface dhcp:
* #configure terminal
* #interface ethernet 0/0
* #ip address dhcp
* #no shutdown
* #exit
* #exit

### interface static:
* #configure terminal
* #interface ethernet 0/0
* #ip adress 192.168.0.2 255.255.255.0
* #no shutdown
* #exit
* #exit
